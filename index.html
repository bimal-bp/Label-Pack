Our State-of-the-Art Facility

Located in the heart of Mumbai's industrial belt, our 15,000 sq.ft. facility houses the latest in packaging technology. We operate with:

  • 6-color flexographic printing machines
  • Automatic label application systems
  • Digital printing capabilities for short runs
  • Quality control lab with spectrophotometers
  • Climate-controlled storage for sensitive materials

Our facility is ISO 9001:2015 certified and follows strict GMP guidelines for all pharmaceutical packaging.
